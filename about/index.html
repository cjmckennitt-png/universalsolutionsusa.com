Our Micromass Concentrate product line delivers professional-grade cleaning and degreasing performance at a fraction of the cost of traditional solutions — highly concentrated, environmentally responsible, and proven in the field across 500+ facilities nationwide.

With over 10 years in business and 30+ years of combined industry experience, we understand what facility managers need: reliable products, consistent supply, and a partner they can count on when it matters.
